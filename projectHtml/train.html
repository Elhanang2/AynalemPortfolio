Train Schedul

This app is used to schedule train time. There are two sections one is form and another schedule display. First the form should be filled with name of train, Destination, starting time, and time per minutes for one trip. The display will display the name of train , Destination, Time to destination, remaining time. It is real time app.The app Is mobile responsive.

Technology used: HTML, CSS, javaScript and jQuery, Firebase
