Trivia Game

This app trivia question for students to evaluate their understanding of mathematics subject. The app can be modified and used to every subject. When the student starts questions, they will be given 30 seconds. If they answered questions with in the given seconds and there answered correct the message will be displayed and continue to the next question. If their answered incorrect result will be displayed next question continue. When the student finished all questions total score, missed questions and unanswered questions will be display. The app is mobile Responsive app

Technology used: HTML, CSS, javaScript and jQuery
