Dog Log

This App Is Used to log shelters Dogs information. when first dogs arrive to shelter they will have unique id . most of the time volunteers come to shelter to walk dogs and log some information in paper. Using this app volunteers can log every information and add some pictures of dogs. This will help to adopters to see all information and some pictures of dog at home. First Time Information Can Be Recordeed By Administrator. When Volunteer Come To Walk Dogs First They Should Have An Acount And They Can Log The Behavior Of Dogs During Walk Time. The app mobile Responsive app

Technology used: React, javascript, nodejs, express and mongoose
